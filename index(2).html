What is Power Washing?

Power washing is a simple cleaning method. It uses water sprayed at very high pressure to clean surfaces. This water pressure is very strong. It can remove dirt, stains, and other unwanted things from your walls, driveways, and outdoor areas. Power washing is also called pressure washing.

The difference between power washing and pressure washing is small. Pressure washing uses only water at high pressure. Power washing uses hot water at high pressure. Hot water works better for tough stains. Both methods make your property very clean.

How Do Power Washing Services Work?

Power washing is a simple process. Here is how it works:

1

Check the Area

The worker arrives at your home. They look at the area that needs cleaning. They check for any delicate surfaces that need special care.

2

Prepare the Space

The worker moves items away from the cleaning area. They cover things that should not get wet. They protect your plants and pets.

3

Start Washing

The worker turns on the power washing machine. The machine sprays water at very high pressure. They move slowly across the surface. The high pressure removes all dirt and stains.

4

Final Check

After washing, the worker looks at the clean area. They make sure everything looks perfect. They clean up and leave your property neat and tidy.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I get power washing done?

Most homes should get power washing done once every year. If you live near trees or in a wet area, you might need it twice a year. Ask your power washing company what they recommend for your home.

2. Is power washing safe for all surfaces?

Power washing is safe for most surfaces like concrete and brick. However, some surfaces like wood and soft stone need lower pressure. Professional workers know which pressure to use for each surface. Always hire a professional to avoid damage.

3. How long does power washing take?

The time depends on how dirty your property is and how large the area is. A small driveway might take 1 to 2 hours. A whole house might take 4 to 6 hours. Ask your company for an estimate.

4. Can I do power washing myself?

You can rent power washing equipment yourself. But it is very heavy and can be dangerous. Professional workers have training and experience. They also have better equipment. It is safer and easier to hire professionals.

5. What is the cost of power washing services in San Antonio, TX?
